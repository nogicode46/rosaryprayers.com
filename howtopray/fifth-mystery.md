# The Fifth Decade

- *Announce the Fifth Mystery.*

> The Fifth Joyful Mystery (Monday, Saturday): **The Finding in the Temple**
> 
> The Fifth Sorrowful Mystery (Tuesday, Friday): **The Crucifixion**
> 
> The Fifth Glorious Mystery (Wednesday, Sunday): **The Coronation of Mary**
> 
> The Fifth Luminous Mystery (Thursday): **The Institution of the Holy Eucharist**
