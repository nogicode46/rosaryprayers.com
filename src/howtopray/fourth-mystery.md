# The Fourth Decade

- *Announce the Fourth Mystery.*

> The Fourth Joyful Mystery (Monday, Saturday): **The Presentation in the Temple**
> 
> The Fourth Sorrowful Mystery (Tuesday, Friday): **The Carrying of the Cross**
> 
> The Fourth Glorious Mystery (Wednesday, Sunday): **The Assumption**
> 
> The Fourth Luminous Mystery (Thursday): **The Transfiguration**

- *Say the Our Father prayer.* 

- *Say ten Hail Marys.* 

- *Say the Glory be to the Father.* 

- *Say the Fatima prayer (optional).*
