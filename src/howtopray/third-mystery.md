# The Third Decade

- *Announce the Third Mystery.*

> The Third Joyful Mystery (Monday, Saturday): **The Nativity**
> 
> The Third Sorrowful Mystery (Tuesday, Friday): **The Crowning with Thorns**
> 
> The Third Glorious Mystery (Wednesday, Sunday): **The Descent of the Holy Spirit**
> 
> The Third Luminous Mystery (Thursday): **Jesus' Proclamation of the Coming of the Kingdom of God**

- *Say the Our Father prayer.* 

- *Say ten Hail Marys.* 

- *Say the Glory be to the Father.* 

- *Say the Fatima prayer (optional).*
