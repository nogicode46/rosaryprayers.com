# The First Decade

- *Announce the First Mystery from one of the Mysteries of the Rosary: Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious, or Luminous. The Mystery selected is usually based on what day of the week it is.*[^1]

> The First Joyful Mystery (Monday, Saturday): **The Annunciation**
> 
> The First Sorrowful Mystery (Tuesday, Friday): **The Agony in the Garden**
> 
> The First Glorious Mystery (Wednesday, Sunday): **The Resurrection**
> 
> The First Luminous Mystery (Thursday): **The Baptism of Christ in the Jordan**

- *Say the Our Father prayer.*  

- *Say ten Hail Marys.*  

- *Say the Glory be to the Father.*  

- *Say the Fatima prayer (optional).*

[^1]: The Five Joyful Mysteries are traditionally prayed on Mondays, Saturdays, and, during the season of Advent, on Sundays. The Five Sorrowful Mysteries are traditionally prayed on Tuesdays, Fridays, and, during the season of Lent, on Sundays.
