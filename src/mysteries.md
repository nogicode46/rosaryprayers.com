# The Mysteries of the Rosary

The spirit of praying the Rosary lies in the meditation upon the fundamental [mysteries](https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10662a.htm) of the faith.[^1]  

The following Mysteries are meditated upon while praying the Rosary:

- [The Joyful Mysteries](joyful/index.html) (Monday, Saturday)  

- [The Sorrowful Mysteries](sorrowful/index.html) (Tuesday, Friday)  

- [The Glorious Mysteries](glorious/index.html) (Wednesday, Sunday)  

- [The Luminous Mysteries](light/index.html) (Thursday)  

During the season of **Advent**, the Joyful Mysteries are prayed on Sundays.  

During the season of **Lent**, the Sorrowful Mysteries are prayed on Sundays.


[^1]: Thurston, Herbert, and Andrew Shipman. "The Rosary." The Catholic Encyclopedia. Vol. 13. New York: Robert Appleton Company, 1912. [http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13184b.htm](http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13184b.htm)
